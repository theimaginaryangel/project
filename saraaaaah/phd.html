    PHD

    PhD in Computer Science

    Our PhD program is designed to prepare students for a career in research and academia. Students will conduct original research under the guidance of experienced faculty, culminating in a dissertation that contributes to the field of computer science.

    Learn More

    Professional Doctorate in Computer Science

    Our Professional Doctorate program is designed for working professionals who want to advance their skills and knowledge of computer science without taking a full-time leave of absence. Students will complete coursework, conduct applied research, and complete a doctoral project that addresses a real-world problem.

    Learn More

    Admissions Requirements

    To be eligible for admission to our doctoral programs, applicants must have:

    • A Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field
    • A strong academic record
    • Research experience or potential
    • A clear statement of purpose outlining their research interests and goals
    • Letters of recommendation from academic or professional references
